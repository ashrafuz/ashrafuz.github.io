Hi! I'm Ashraf
...a workaholic game programmer/developer!

I am currently working at Ulka Games as a game developer. Although many of my hours are dedicated to my job and I cannot share most of my current projects, I wanted to showcase my previous casual/hobby projects as my portfolio. This should give you an idea about myself as a developer.
NB: I would like to say, Please do not take these as my highest calibre because I am constantly learning and evolving. And some of the projects below are done very early in my game dev journey, I have just made sure it runs on latest platform. I have given approximate timing of the projects in title with the hope for your kind & just consideration.
